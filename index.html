Intro

Hi, I am Bibek. I like solving problems and learning new things every day. I spend most of my time building new Java applications, conducting research on Federated Learning Technique, and learn about new technology such as quantum computing. I am a college student, who is really passionate about computer science and it's vast applications in real life. By the way check out my weekly blog on AI and quantum computing Quantum Computing For Beginners.

During my free time, I like working with kids. In fact, I am a founder of a mentoring club on campus "Hope Club". Just as the name suggest, it works with first generation high school students from immigrant background in Bridgeport by providing them the resources and guidance to apply to college. In my free time, I like playing soccer, competing in coding competitions, and hiking.
